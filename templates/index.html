Your Trusted Agricultural Partner

Located conveniently next to Meru University of Science and Technology, we are committed to supporting both small-scale and large-scale farmers with the best products and services in the agricultural sector.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

+

We are open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Do you offer delivery services?

+

Yes, we offer delivery services within Nchiru and surrounding areas for orders above KSh 2,000. Delivery charges may apply depending on the distance.

How often should I vaccinate my poultry?

+

Poultry vaccination schedules vary depending on the vaccine. Generally, Newcastle disease vaccine is given at day 1, week 2, and every 2-3 months thereafter. Our veterinary staff can provide a customized vaccination schedule for your flock.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, M-Pesa, and bank transfers. Our M-Pesa paybill number is 123456, account number being your name.

Do you offer credit facilities?

+

We offer credit facilities to our registered customers with good payment history. Terms and conditions apply. Please visit our store with your national ID and two references to apply.

Can I return products I'm not satisfied with?

+

We accept returns of unopened and unused products within 7 days of purchase with the original receipt. Perishable goods and veterinary drugs cannot be returned for health and safety reasons.
